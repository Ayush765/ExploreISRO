EOS-03

EOS-03 is a state-of-the-art agile Earth observation satellite which will be placed in a Geosynchronous Transfer Orbit by GSLV-F10. Subsequently, the satellite will reach the final geostationary orbit using its onboard propulsion system.

EOS-03 Mission Specifications

Parameter Specification
Satellite Name EOS-3
Launch Date November 7, 2021
Launch Vehicle PSLV-C50
Launch Site Satish Dhawan Space Centre, Sriharikota, India
Manufacturer Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
Mission Type Earth Observation
Orbit Type Sun-Synchronous Orbit (SSO)
Weight Approximately 1,200 kg
Dimensions 2m x 2m x 2m
Communication Band X-band, S-band
Power Source Solar Panels
Resolution 1 meter
Spectral Bands RGB, Infrared, Microwave
Swath Width Up to 30 km
Revisit Time 5 days
Data Storage Capacity 2 TB onboard storage
Operational Lifetime 5 years
Primary Users ISRO, Environmental and Agricultural researchers
Additional Information EOS-3 is equipped with advanced imaging technology for high-resolution Earth observation. It aims to provide valuable data for environmental monitoring, agriculture, disaster response, and urban planning.
